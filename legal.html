Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a legitimate draw?

Yes. This is a demonstration website, but in a real-world scenario, we would be a legally registered entity operating under all applicable laws for prize competitions. All draws are conducted fairly and transparently.

How is the winner selected?

The winner is selected using a third-party, independently verified random number generator to ensure complete fairness. The draw is recorded and can be made available upon request.

What if I win?

If you are the lucky winner, our team will contact you directly via the email and phone number you provided during checkout. We will then arrange for the secure, insured delivery of your prize to your address, anywhere in the world.

Fair Draw Information

We are committed to 100% transparency and fairness. Our draws are conducted under the following principles:

  • Limited Entries: Each draw has a fixed number of entries. Once the maximum is reached, the draw is closed and scheduled.
  • Verified Random Selection: We use a certified third-party service (like Random.org) to generate the winning entry number. This process is automated and tamper-proof.
  • Public Announcement: The winner will be announced on our website and social media channels after they have been successfully contacted and have confirmed their prize.
